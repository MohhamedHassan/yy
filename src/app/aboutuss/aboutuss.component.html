Helping the disabled
through volunteers

Help Me is a free app that connects the disabled and unable
to volunteers who are able to get help by sending a request

How it Works

Every day, able volunteers help solve tasks big and small
to help the disabled lead a more independent life.

A disabled or incapable person asks for help

As a disabled or incapable person, whenever you need help, our volunteers are happy to help. By agreeing to the request, Which you sent to him, and it has the type of service, date and time. You and a volunteer can communicate directly and solve a problem through the phone number you sent in the request for assistance. The volunteer will help you solve your problem

A volunteer who is able to receive assistance from a disabled person

As a capable volunteer, you can help just by going on the website or installing the Help Me app. A disabled or incapable user may need help with anything, moving around in new surroundings, etc.
