Directions

When a request comes to you, it appears on the Accept orders page. When you click on the Accept button, the request will be transferred to the disabled with the done button. If the disabled presses this button, it means that you have done the service and the number of points for this service will be added to you, and you will have a specific target when you reach it, you will achieve 5 stars

When you send a request, this request goes to all volunteers, and when one of the volunteers accepts, you get a notification on the Accept orders page. When the volunteer completes this service, you will click on the done button in order for the points of this service to go to him
